Bill Nye

About the GOAT

William Sanford Nye is an American science communicator, comedian, author, and inventor. He's best known for hosting the Emmy award-winning educational show Bill Nye the Science Guy, which aired from 1993-1998. Nye's passion for science education continues through his work as CEO of The Planetary Society, a nonprofit that promotes space exploration and environmental stewardship
